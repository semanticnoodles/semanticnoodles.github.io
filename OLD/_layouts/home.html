Ph.D. Research Area & Topics

I am researching the ‘ethical requirements’ for documentation that accompanies digitized cultural heritage collections as data, in relation to their reuse through Artificial Intelligence systems.

Core Topics

  • Cultural Heritage / Digital Preservation
  • Critical Data Studies / Data Feminism
  • Artificial Intelligence / Computer Vision

About Me

In 2014, I obtained my MSc in Quaternary, Prehistory, and Archaeology at the University of Ferrara (Italy). The same year, I got involved in the excavation at the Terramara of Pilastri, where I had the opportunity to work as a Digital and Community Archaeologist until 2020. After spending a year researching digital humanities at the Cyprus University of Technology, I moved to Ireland after winning the Ph.D. position I currently hold.
